Parent-Child Tennis Program

Bond with your child while learning tennis together!

Play Tennis With Your Child!

The Vernon Tennis Association is excited to offer a special program where parents can learn tennis while playing with their children (ages 10+).

Program Details:

  • When: Every Monday from 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM
  • Where: Marshall's Fields
  • Who: Parents with children 10 years old and over
  • Cost: TBA

Our Fast-Track Learning Program

Never played tennis before? No problem! Our specially designed program will help you become a tennis player in just two weeks. You'll learn:

  • Basic strokes and techniques
  • Court positioning and movement
  • Scoring and rules of the game
  • Fun drills to practice with your child

Why Join Our Program?

This is a unique opportunity to:

  • Spend quality time with your child
  • Learn a new sport together
  • Get active and healthy as a family
  • Meet other parents in the community
  • Develop skills that will last a lifetime
